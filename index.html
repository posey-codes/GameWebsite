Introduction

We at Rat Studios are dedicated to creating quality games that we pour our heart and soul into. We work tirelessly to provide the best experiences possible for our players. Our experiences in the game design field alongside our passion for the craft allow us to create the most amazing worlds for our players. I hope you will enjoy the journeys that we put you in.


Game Engine

Almost all of our work will be done in the Unity Engine. We believe Unity is the best tool for the job, it is a powerful game engine that uses the high-level language of C#. We believe this gives us more flexibility and ease of access as opposed to something like the Unreal engine which uses C++. C++ while a fantastic programming language puts in unneedless complications into the development process.
